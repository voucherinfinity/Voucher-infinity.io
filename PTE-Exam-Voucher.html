PTE Exam Eligibility

There are no specific criteria set by the Pearson PLC Group – the conducting body of the PTE exam. Though, according to the PTE exam eligibility criteria, the following things should be considered.

Education Qualification Required for PTE Academic Exam

Pearson PLC Group has not compiled any education qualification criteria for candidates seeking to take the PTE Academic test.

Minimum Age Limit to Appear for PTE Exam

To take the PTE test, the applicant must be at least 16 years old. Applicants below 18 years of age are required to take a parental consent form signed by parents before appearing for the test. Candidates can download the consent form from the official site of the PTE Exam.

Types of PTE Exam

The Pearson Language Tests- a part of Pearson Group conducts three formats of English proficiency test to evaluate the language skills of students. The three types of PTE tests are PTE Academic, PTE Academic UKVI, and PTE Home.

PTE Exam Pattern

PTE Academic Syllabus is sectioned into 3 parts – Speaking & Writing, Reading, and Listening. Candidates get marked on these three parts and you get the PTE Academic total score as well. PTE Exam Registration

Aspirants seeking to register for the PTE exam would be required to follow the steps mentioned to book their PTE exam slot. Candidates should note that for booking your PTE exam online, you need a valid Identification – a valid Indian passport. PTE Exam Day Tips

Candidates looking to appear for the PTE Academic Exam can go through the following instructions mentioned below.

  • It is suggested that you should arrive 30 mins before the reporting time at the PTE Academic Exam center as this would enable them to arrive on time and avoid any last-minute hassle.
  • There should be no point of missing breakfast. A moderately heavy breakfast would energize you for a longer period of time. Candidates are suggested to keep a light snack or a fruit with them.
  • Candidates are advised to wear loose clothes that are comfortable in all temperatures. It is very important for the students to feel comfortable while taking the examination.
  • Once after arrival at the PTE Test Centre candidates would be required to show their id proof which should be a valid passport to proceed ahead. The candidate would have his/her photograph taken and biometrics scanned at the time of verification of id for official purposes only.

PTE Results and Score

PTE Results are generally available online within 2 working days of the exam date. The PTE Academic results are available online. Students can access their result through PTE Academic student account. Firstly, you will get an email within five working days containing instructions on accessing the results online. After that, you are required to follow the guidelines to log in to your Pearson account. Candidates can share their results with the colleges and universities of their choice via Pearson account.

PTE Academic Exam is scored on a scale of 10 – 90 where 10 is the lowest, with increments of 1 point. The score shows the person’s skills to read, speak, understand verbal instructions and write the English language. The scores are displayed in a graph, providing you an easy understanding of the core skills in the language as well as improvement areas.

Validity of the PTE Test Scores

The scores of your PTE Academic exam are valid for a period of 2 years.
