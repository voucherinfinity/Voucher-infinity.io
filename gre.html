GRE Exam Voucher

January 1, 2021 by Mark

GRE (Graduate Record Examination) Test is one of the widely accepted tests conducted by ETS (Educational Testing Service) that is used to take admission in graduate programs in thousands of universities and institutions overseas. Aspirants seeking admission to the world’s top business schools or keen to pursue their master’s or MBA courses appear for the GRE exam. The GRE evaluates the analytical writing, quantitative ability, and verbal reasoning skills of candidates. GRE Test Fee

The fee for taking the GRE exam is $205 (USD), though this may vary depending on the region. Additional fees may apply for services like rescheduling appointments or obtaining score reports.

GRE Test Format

The GRE exam consists of three main sections:

  • Verbal Reasoning: This section evaluates the test-taker's ability to analyze and evaluate written material, as well as synthesize information and comprehend relationships among words and concepts.
  • Quantitative Reasoning: This section assesses the test-taker's ability to understand quantitative information, interpret and analyze data, solve problems using mathematical models, and apply mathematical concepts.
  • Analytical Writing: This section requires test-takers to critically analyze and evaluate an issue and present a well-reasoned argument or analyze an argument presented in a passage.

GRE Test Registration

To register for the GRE exam, follow these steps:

  • Create an ETS (Educational Testing Service) account on the official GRE website.
  • Select a test date and location based on availability.
  • Complete the registration process.
  • Pay the exam fee using the GRE voucher code or other payment methods.
  • Receive confirmation of your registration and test details via email.


GRE Test Day Tips

Here are some tips for test day:

  • Arrive early at the test center to avoid any last-minute stress.

  • Bring valid identification (passport or government-issued ID) as required.
  • Stay calm and focused during the exam.
  • Utilize the provided breaks strategically to stay refreshed.
  • Follow all test center rules and instructions.

GRE Score Reporting

GRE scores are typically available online within 10-15 days after the test date. Test-takers can view their unofficial scores immediately after completing the exam. Official score reports are sent to designated institutions chosen during the registration process.

GRE Score Validity

GRE scores are valid for five years from the test date. After this period, scores are no longer reportable.
