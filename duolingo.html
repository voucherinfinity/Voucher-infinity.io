January 1, 2021 by Mark

Full Form of DET - The Duolingo English Test. The Duolingo English Test (DET) is a computer-based English language proficiency test that evaluates your skills in speaking, reading, writing, and listening, ensuring your ability to study in an English-speaking country. If you're considering taking the DET exam, you can be confident that it's a widely recognized computer-based test accepted by institutions worldwide. To assist you in your exam journey, Voucher Infinity Education provides discounted DET Exam vouchers at competitive prices, ensuring affordability without compromising quality.

DET Exam Voucher:

The Duolingo English Test (DET) exam voucher is a 12-digit alphanumeric code used to pay for the DET exam fees. The DET voucher code, or DET promo code, enables you to book the DET exam at a reduced price compared to the standard exam fee.

DET Exam Voucher Usage:

It serves as one of the payment methods to cover your DET exam fee when booking the exam online through the exam booking portal. Instead of conventional payment methods, you simply enter the DET voucher code to avail of the discount on the DET exam fee. The DET voucher covers the full cost of the exam.

DET Exam Main Highlights:

  • Exam Name: DET
  • DET stands for: Duolingo English Test
  • Official Website: https://englishtest.duolingo.com/
  • Popular for: English Proficiency Assessment
  • Accepted by: Universities globally
  • Mode of Exam: Online
  • DET Fee: $59
  • Skills Tested: English Speaking, Writing, Listening, and Reading


DET Exam Eligibility

There are no specific eligibility criteria set by Duolingo for the DET exam. However, certain considerations should be taken into account:

  • Education Qualification Required for DET: No specific requirements.

  • Minimum Age Limit for DET Exam: Typically, applicants must be at least 16 years old. Applicants under 18 may need parental consent.

Types of DET Exam:

The Duolingo English Test offers a single format for evaluating language proficiency.

DET Exam Pattern

The DET exam assesses skills in speaking, writing, listening, and reading. Candidates are evaluated based on their performance in these areas, and scores range from 10 to 160.


DET Exam Registration

To register for the DET exam, follow these steps:

  • Visit the official Duolingo English Test website.
  • Create your account by providing necessary details.
  • Once registered, schedule your exam date and time.
  • Pay the exam fee using the DET voucher code or other payment methods.
  • Finalize your exam booking.

DET Exam Day Tips

Candidates appearing for the DET exam should consider the following tips

  • Arrive at the test center early to avoid any last-minute stress.
  • Ensure you have had breakfast to maintain energy levels.
  • Dress comfortably for the exam.
  • Bring a valid ID, usually a passport, for verification purposes.

DET Results and Score

DET results are typically available online within a few days after the exam. Scores range from 10 to 160, reflecting proficiency in English language skills. Scores are accessible through the Duolingo English Test student account, and they remain valid for two years.

Validity of DET Test Scores

DET exam scores are valid for a period of two years.
