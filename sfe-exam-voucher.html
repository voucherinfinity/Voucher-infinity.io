PSI SELT (UKVI) Structure

The aspirants can opt for two skills (Speaking & Listening) or four skills (Listening, Reading, Writing & Speaking) exam. This exam option varies on the basis of visa and purpose for the UK.

  • SFE (Speaking & Listening) exam is a short online conversation with a live interlocutor.
  • SFE (Listening, Reading, Writing & Speaking) exam consists of two sections:
  • Written section includes which is taken on a computer
  • Spoken section includes an online interview with live Interlocutors
